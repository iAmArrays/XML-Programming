Welcome to The Apes! website. The Apes! are the hottest band on the planet right now with such hits as "Dude, Who Took My Banana?" and who could forget their biggest hit, the love ballad "Please Don't Throw That at Me". This website's goal is to provide all fans with a little bit more information about the band and the loyal "Little Apes" who keep them on top!

Disclaimer! This service is not intended for the those with criminal intent. The Apes! are a lot like people (you could almost call them cousins) so their privacy should be respected.
